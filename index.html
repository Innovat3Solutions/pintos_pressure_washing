FAQ

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know before booking your first clean.

How often should I pressure wash my home?

Most homes benefit from a yearly wash, but timing depends on your climate, tree coverage, and the type of siding.

Is pressure washing safe for all surfaces?

We adjust pressure and technique for each surface. Soft washes for siding and roofs, stronger for concrete and masonry.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we're fully licensed and insured for every job, so you have complete peace of mind.

Do you offer free estimates?

Absolutely. All estimates are free and come with no obligation. Call us or submit the quote form and we'll get back to you fast.

Do I need to be home during the service?

No. As long as we have access to water and the areas being cleaned, you don't need to be on-site. We'll send before and after photos when we're done.

How long does a typical job take?

Most residential jobs take between 2 and 5 hours depending on square footage and the services booked. We'll give you an exact estimate upfront.

What cleaning solutions do you use?

We use professional-grade, biodegradable solutions that are safe for pets, plants, and your family.

Will pressure washing damage my plants or landscaping?

No. We pre-wet and rinse surrounding landscaping before and after each job to protect your greenery.

What areas do you service?

We service homes and businesses throughout the local region. Not sure if we cover your area? Just ask. We're happy to confirm.

How do I pay?

We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment is due after the job is complete and you're satisfied with the results.
